## After Filing

1. You'll get a confirmation with your **File Number** — save this
2. The Secretary of State processes filings within 3-5 business days (online is faster)
3. Once approved, order a **certified copy** ($30) — you'll need it for your bank account and grant registrations
4. Your LLC is now officially formed — move on to the EIN application

---

## Filing Strategy: Members Now or Later?

**Option A — File with all members now:**
- Cleaner from the start
- Requires all member names and addresses before filing
- All members listed from day one

**Option B — File with just William Brooks Parker, amend later:**
- Gets the LLC formed faster (you can file TODAY)
- File an amendment ($30) to add members once their info is confirmed
- Partner 4 (to be named later) makes this approach practical
- **Recommended given the urgency of grant deadlines**

If you go with Option B, file as a single-member manager-managed LLC now, then amend the Articles and update the Operating Agreement when all members are confirmed.
