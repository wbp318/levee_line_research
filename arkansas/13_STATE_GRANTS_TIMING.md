# State Grants Operational Manual — Timing, Earmarks, Clawbacks

Execution reference (not primer) for every state/federal funding program relevant to LLRC. For each program: application-to-decision time, decision-to-cash flow, use-of-funds restrictions, reporting burden, clawback triggers, pre-commitment requirements, and April 2026 status.

Read this before signing any lease, hiring anyone, or spending a dollar on research expenses.

---

## Section 0 — Six Foundational Rules (Miss One, Lose Everything)

Under Arkansas's Consolidated Incentive Act of 2003 (Act 182, as amended; rules at 168.00.05 Ark. Code R. § 001) and related statutes, six rules govern nearly every AEDC incentive below. Read these first.

### Rule 1 — The pre-commitment trap is real

An eligible business **MUST submit an application and project plan to AEDC *prior to incurring project costs and hiring new employees* associated with the project.** Spending or hiring before the Financial Incentive Agreement (FIA) is signed eliminates those costs/jobs from the credit base — **permanently, with no retroactive remedy.**

**This applies to:** Advantage Arkansas, ArkPlus, Create Rebate, InvestArk, Targeted Business R&D, Strategic Value R&D, University-Based R&D, In-House R&D.

### Rule 2 — "Discretionary" means AEDC Executive Director decides

Every meaningful credit (except Advantage Arkansas payroll credit, which is earn-by-formula) is discretionary. A company with $500 in capital and no partners signed will not get discretionary offers. AEDC negotiates harder when you can show equity commitment, payroll projections, and a real partner roster.

### Rule 3 — Targeted Business status is the gateway (and LLRC doesn't qualify yet)

"Agriculture, food and environmental sciences" is one of AEDC's six targeted sectors. But to qualify:

1. **Proof of $250,000 equity investment** (partner contributions + first SBIR award may count — confirm with AEDC project manager)
2. **Wages of 150% of the lesser of state or county average hourly wage**
3. **Sector fit** (ag-AI qualifies)

**At $500 current capital, LLRC does not yet qualify.** The roadmap must bridge to $250K equity via partner contributions, angel investment, or a first federal SBIR award. **Until Targeted Business is approved, the fallback is Strategic Value R&D credit (33%, same cap, non-transferable) or 20% non-targeted In-House.**

### Rule 4 — Chicot County = Tier 4 (max benefit tier)

Only tier where the **$50K annual new-payroll threshold** applies (vs. $125K in Tier 1). Unlocks 4% Advantage Arkansas rate + Create Rebate access + best ArkPlus terms.

### Rule 5 — Arkansas state FY runs July 1 – June 30

Caps reset July 1. The **EITC $6.25M annual cap is first-come, first-served.** July–September is the safest window to file. Filing in May–June risks cap exhaustion.

### Rule 6 — R&D credits are wages-only; structural difference matters

All four AR R&D credits (Targeted 33%, Strategic Value 33%, University 33%, In-House 20%) cover **in-house wages + fringe benefits only** — NOT supplies, equipment, cloud compute, or buildings. Federal IRC §41 QRE definitions apply.

Also critical structural difference:

- **20% In-House credit** applies only to R&D spending *in excess of prior-year baseline* (incremental). Y1 has no baseline → full 20% × Y1 QREs. Y2+ requires year-over-year increases.
- **33% Targeted Business / Strategic Value / University credits** apply to entire qualified amount (no baseline).

Worth >$10K/yr structural difference.

## Section 1 — Arkansas State Programs

### 1. AEDC SBIR Matching Grant

Up to $50K Phase I / $100K Phase II. Authority: Act 166 of 2017; §15-3-601.

| Data Point | Detail |
|------------|--------|
| Amount | Up to 50% of federal SBIR award, capped at **$50K Phase I / $100K Phase II** |
| App → decision | Not publicly posted. Practitioner expectation: **30–60 days** after federal SBIR award confirmation |
| Decision → cash | **Reimbursement basis.** Submit invoices + financial reports; 30–45 days per reimbursement cycle |
| Use of funds | Commercialization activities tied to the federal SBIR project. **≥51% of matched funds must be spent in Arkansas** |
| Reporting | Invoices + financial reports per reimbursement cycle; final report upon full expenditure documenting 51% AR spend |
| Clawback | Failure to document 51% AR spend or to submit final report triggers recovery |
| Pre-commitment | Requires **federal SBIR award first** — cannot pre-register |
| Status Apr 2026 | Active (SBIR/STTR federal authorization lapse may delay upstream federal awards — see master caveat in doc 11) |

URL: https://www.arkansasedc.com/science-technology/division/commercialization/sbir-matching-grant

### 2. Technology Transfer Assistance Grant (TTAG)

Up to $5,000 project total.

| Data Point | Detail |
|------------|--------|
| Amount | **$3,750 AEDC + $1,250 applicant = $5,000 project ceiling**. First $2,500 fully AEDC-funded; next $2,500 is 50/50 match |
| App → decision | Reviewed **every 2 weeks**. Submit by Friday before review meeting. Decision typically **≤14 days** |
| Decision → cash | Funds flow to the **resource provider** (university, lab, consultant), not to LLRC. You receive the *service*, not cash |
| Use of funds | Engineering/technical support fees, database searches, travel, **SBIR/STTR proposal prep**, feasibility consulting |
| Reporting | Project must complete within **90 days**. Final deliverable to AEDC |
| Clawback | Minimal — grant is small and paid only on invoice |
| Pre-commitment | Yes — must be awarded **before** engaging the resource provider |
| Status Apr 2026 | Active. Limit **2 projects per enterprise per state fiscal year** |

**Strategic use:** Pair with UAPB or UA Division of Agriculture for SBIR proposal-writing help. Fastest cash-in-service in the entire portfolio.

URL: https://www.arkansasedc.com/Manufacturing-Solutions/division/consulting-services/technology-transfer-assistance-grant

### 3. AR Targeted Business In-House R&D Tax Credit — 33%

**Structurally best credit in the portfolio, but requires Targeted Business designation.**

| Data Point | Detail |
|------------|--------|
| Authority | §15-4-2708; Targeted Business provisions |
| Amount | **33% of qualified R&D expenditures, capped $50K/yr** for up to 5 years following signed FIA |
| Transferable | **Yes, once** — sold within 1 year of issuance. Secondary market rate: **85–92¢ on the dollar** |
| App → decision | Submit **45 days before company tax year-end**. Discretionary — allow 60–90 days for ED review |
| Decision → cash | Tax credit certificate issued by ASTA/AEDC, attached to AR income tax return. Cash equivalent on tax filing OR via sale |
| Use of funds | **Wages + fringe benefits only** for in-house R&D employees (FT permanent or contractual). Supplies, equipment, buildings NOT covered |
| Reporting | Annual project plan updates; annual QRE certification to AEDC; offset claim on AR1100CT |
| Clawback | FIA contains recapture clauses: moving out of state, failing to maintain operations, misrepresenting QREs |
| Pre-commitment | **FIA must be signed BEFORE R&D spending begins.** Wages paid before FIA execution are NOT in the credit base |
| Status Apr 2026 | Active. **Requires Targeted Business designation** ($250K equity + 150% wage threshold + sector fit — see Rule 3) |
| Carryforward | 9 years; offsets 100% of liability |

URL: https://www.arkansasedc.com/science-technology/division/commercialization/research-development-tax-credits

### 4. AR Strategic Value R&D Tax Credit — 33% (fallback if not Targeted Business yet)

| Data Point | Detail |
|------------|--------|
| Amount | 33% of QREs, capped **$50K/yr for 5 years** from FIA signing |
| Transferable | **NO** (unlike Targeted Business credit — key difference) |
| Use of funds | In-house wages for research in fields of "long-term economic/commercial value to AR" per AEDC-approved plan |

| Decision → cash | Tax credit certificate; used on AR1100CT |
| Clawback / Pre-commitment | Same FIA framework as Targeted Business. **Pre-commitment required** |
| Status Apr 2026 | Active. **Primary fallback for emerging firms not yet Targeted Business–designated** |

**Strategic note:** If LLRC cannot clear the $250K equity bar on Day 1, Strategic Value is the 33% path forward until the Targeted Business application is ready.

### 5. AR University-Based R&D Tax Credit — 33%

Activated by partnerships with AR universities — **exactly what we want with UAPB / UAM / UADA**.

| Data Point | Detail |
|------------|--------|
| Amount | **33% of qualified expenditures under written contract with an AR college/university or AR-based research org**, approved by AEDC Executive Director in writing |
| Transferable | Yes, if held by a Targeted Business (one sale, within 1 year of issuance) |
| App → decision | Submit 45 days pre-FYE; reviewed by ASTA |
| Decision → cash | Certificate of Tax Credit issued by ASTA, attached to AR return |
| Use of funds | Wages + fringe for in-house research employees **plus** contract research paid to UAPB, UA Division of Agriculture, ASU, UCA, UAM, etc. |
| Pre-commitment | **Contract must be pre-approved in writing** by AEDC Executive Director. Retroactive approval is not granted |
| Status Apr 2026 | Active; 9-year carryforward |

**Double-dip with UAPB:** University contract activates this 33% credit AND positions UAPB as a 1890 CBG sub-award partner (see #25).

### 6. AR In-House R&D Tax Credit (non-targeted fallback) — 20%

| Data Point | Detail |
|------------|--------|
| Amount | **20% of QREs in excess of the prior-year baseline** (incremental only) for 5 years from FIA |
| Gotcha | **Requires federal R&D tax credit filing (IRC §41)** in prior year as a precondition |
| Structural trap | Y1 has no baseline → full 20% × Y1 QRE. Y2+ requires year-over-year QRE increases to earn more credit |
| Transferable | NO — corporate income tax only |
| Use of funds | Qualifying R&D wages + fringe only |
| Pre-commitment | Yes — FIA required before any R&D spending |
| Status Apr 2026 | Active; 9-yr carryforward; offsets 100% of liability |

### 7. Advantage Arkansas Payroll Tax Credit — 4% Tier 4

| Data Point | Detail |
|------------|--------|
| Amount | **4% of payroll for new FT permanent employees** (Tier 4 rate). **+1% bonus** if avg hourly wage exceeds 125% of lesser of county/state avg wage |
| Threshold | Chicot (Tier 4) requires **$50K new annual payroll** to trigger. Avg wage must be ≥ greater of county avg hourly OR **$12.50/hr** |
| App → decision | File with AEDC before hiring; ~30–60 days for FIA execution |
| Decision → cash | Income tax credit (NOT cash). Earned each year new payroll is maintained |
| Reporting | Annual payroll certification; DFA Form AR1100RC5 |
| Clawback | If jobs not maintained for full 5-year earn period, credits earned on lost jobs may be recaptured. **Relocation = forfeit** |
| Pre-commitment | **FIA must be signed BEFORE hiring** the employees whose payroll is claimed. Hiring before FIA = zero credit on those employees |
| Status Apr 2026 | Active; non-discretionary (earn-by-formula) once FIA signed |
| Carryforward | 9 years |

**Owner-operator caveat** — see doc 11 Part III §4. Partnership tax law (Rev. Rul. 69-184) blocks W-2 self-hire; AEDC requires employees be AR taxpayers. Budget Advantage AR around future AR-resident FTE hires, not self-payroll.

### 8. Create Rebate — Cash rebate, up to 10 years

**Only program that pays actual cash (not credits) annually.** Unrestricted use.

| Data Point | Detail |
|------------|--------|
| Standard threshold | **$2M new annual payroll within 24 months** of FIA; wages ≥ 150% of lesser of state/county avg hourly wage → 5% rebate up to 10 years |
| Tech-based variant | **$250K** minimum new annual payroll; wages **≥ 175%** of lesser of state/county avg hourly wage → **5% rebate** |
| App → decision | Fully discretionary (AEDC Exec Director). 60–120 days typical for negotiation |
| Decision → cash | **Annual cash payment from AR General Revenue.** Not credit — actual cash wire. 3–6 months from year-end to wire |
| Use of funds | **Unrestricted** — rebate, not a grant |
| Reporting | Annual payroll certification; ongoing FIA compliance |
| Clawback | If payroll threshold not maintained, AEDC can terminate and pursue recovery of prior-year rebates. **Relocation = full recapture** |
| Pre-commitment | FIA before hiring |
| Status Apr 2026 | Active; discretionary (AEDC can say no based on FY capacity) |

**Realistic LLRC target:** Tech variant. Chicot avg hourly wage ≈ $17–$19/hr → 175% ≈ $30–$33/hr → ~$62K–$69K FTE salaries. **4 FTEs at ~$65K each** hits the $250K threshold. Annual rebate ~$12,500/yr cash × 10 years = **$125K total cash**.

### 9. Equity Investment Tax Credit (EITC) — 33⅓%, transferable

**Best single tool for partner/angel capital raise.**

| Data Point | Detail |
|------------|--------|
| Amount | **33⅓% of investor's cash investment** (equity or convertible note) into an AEDC-approved targeted business. Offsets 50% of investor's AR income tax liability/yr |
| State cap | **$6.25M/yr total**; first-come, first-served. July–Sept is safest window |
| App → decision | Business must first be AEDC-approved as eligible; each investment reported within tax year. 30–60 days |
| Decision → cash | Credit flows to **investor** (not company). Transferable once within 1 year. Market rate 85–92¢ on dollar |
| Use of funds | Investor cash is unrestricted to LLRC. Credit is the sweetener paid to investor |
| Reporting | Annual investment certification |
| Clawback | If LLRC fails to maintain targeted-business status, investor credit for that year may be rescinded |
| Pre-commitment | **Company must be approved as eligible BEFORE investor writes check** |
| Status Apr 2026 | Active. Watch the $6.25M cap |
| Carryforward | 9 years |

**Partner raise math:** $250K partner equity → ~$83K in transferable AR credits at 33⅓%, saleable for ~$70K–$75K cash to the investor. **Real sweetener on capital raises.**

URL: https://www.arkansasedc.com/why-arkansas/business-climate/incentives/pages/equity-investment-tax-credit

### 10. InvestArk — **NOT APPLICABLE**

Requires **2 years continuous AR operation + $5M+ investment at a single site**. New LLC cannot qualify until ~April 2028 at earliest. Capital threshold irrelevant for R&D startup. **Skip.**

### 11. ArkPlus — Low-leverage for LLRC

10% income tax credit on total investment; discretionary. Thresholds favor capital equipment buys. For a services R&D LLC with minimal physical investment, this is low-leverage. **Skip unless capital equipment spend exceeds ~$500K.**

### 12. Arkansas Rural Community Grant — **NOT APPLICABLE**

Funds fire trucks, community centers, storm sirens for incorporated towns <3,000 population. LLRC is a for-profit LLC and ineligible. Related **Arkansas Community & Economic Development Grant Program (ACEDP)** flows through municipalities; Lake Village could theoretically apply on LLRC's behalf for infrastructure, but that requires the city as applicant. **Skip direct application.**

## Section 9 — Pre-Commitment Trap Summary

**Programs that REQUIRE an executed AEDC agreement BEFORE spending, hiring, or signing a lease:**

| Trigger Event | Must Be Signed First | Source |
|---------------|---------------------|--------|
| **Hiring first new FT employee** | Advantage Arkansas FIA, Create Rebate FIA, ArkPlus FIA | §15-4-2705, §15-4-2707 |
| **First dollar of R&D wages** | Targeted Business R&D FIA, Strategic Value R&D FIA, In-House R&D FIA, University-Based R&D FIA | §15-4-2708 |
| **Investor wiring equity to claim EITC** | EITC eligibility approval (business side) | §15-4-2301 et seq. |
| **First capital equipment purchase** (if InvestArk/ArkPlus) | InvestArk/ArkPlus FIA | §15-4-2706 |
| **Signed university research contract** (to claim University R&D credit) | Written AEDC Executive Director approval of the contract | §15-4-2708(c) |
| **Federal SBIR spending (AR match)** | Federal SBIR award notice (not AEDC agreement — but federal SBIR is the gate) | §15-3-601 |

**NO pre-commitment trap** for: TTAG, EITC (investor side only), ADFA SSBCI lender programs, Louisiana programs, USDA/DRA/NIFA federal grants (beyond normal pre-award cost rules).

## Section 10 — Don't-Miss Checklist Before Any R&D Spending Begins

**Execute in this order. Missing any step forfeits credits on that expenditure permanently.**

1. **Form the LLC + get EIN** (AR LL-01 filed; EIN same-day online)
2. **Register on SAM.gov with UEI** — required for everything federal and AEDC SBIR Match
3. **Execute written consulting/employment agreements** with founder as PI *before* paying any R&D wages — retroactive documentation is not accepted
4. **Submit AEDC application + project plan** to Division of Science & Technology (501-682-5996) specifying intent to claim R&D credit (Targeted Business or Strategic Value). NAICS 541715; Agriculture, Food & Environmental Sciences targeted sector
5. **Get AEDC Executive Director discretionary approval letter** — gating document
6. **Sign the Financial Incentive Agreement (FIA).** Do NOT:
   - Pay the first R&D wage dollar
   - Sign the first lease
   - Hire the first W-2 employee

   until the FIA is countersigned and dated.
7. **Simultaneously file Advantage Arkansas FIA** — AEDC often handles R&D + Advantage Arkansas on the same packet for Tier 4 counties
8. **If partnering with UAPB / UA Division of Agriculture:** Get AEDC Executive Director's written pre-approval of the university contract BEFORE the university invoices for any research
9. **Track every QRE by project code from day 1** — AR QREs mirror federal IRC §41. No contemporaneous documentation → AEDC won't certify the credit amount at FYE
10. **File federal R&D credit (Form 6765) for Year 1** — precondition for 20% In-House AR credit in Year 2
11. **Submit R&D credit application 45 days before FYE** to ASTA/AEDC for certificate issuance
12. **For EITC:** AEDC eligibility approval must be issued *before* the investor writes the check. Credit attaches to the investor; they will walk if the eligibility letter isn't in hand
13. **Open books with a CPA experienced in AR AEDC credits** — short list: Landmark CPAs, Frost PLLC, Hogan Taylor (Little Rock). Annual compliance certification is not DIY

|---------|-------------|
| Advantage Arkansas | Prior-year credits stand if already earned and used; forfeit all unearned future credits. Jobs-lost recapture clause in most FIAs |
| Targeted Business R&D (33%) | Recapture under FIA breach clause — AEDC can void FIA and demand prior credit-equivalent payment. Sold credits generally remain with buyer, but AEDC may pursue company for equivalent cash |
| Strategic Value R&D | Same as above |
| Create Rebate | **Full recapture of all prior-year cash rebates** under standard FIA terms if company ceases AR operations |
| EITC | Investor credits generally stand once issued; company loses future eligibility |
| AEDC SBIR Match | 51% AR spend requirement must be met at final report; relocating before that point forfeits unreimbursed balance |
| SSBCI loans | Default under loan covenants; lender pursues collateral/guaranty. No state clawback beyond loan |
| DRA SEDAP | Federal award recapture per 2 CFR Part 200 |

**All AEDC FIAs contain a "material breach" clause** naming relocation, cessation of operations, and falsification as triggers. Read the specific FIA presented to you — terms vary by offer.

## Corrections to Doc 11 (Arkansas Expansion) Based on This Research

- **Advantage Arkansas wage floor** was stated as $15.99 or $16.75/hr in earlier versions. Correct figure: **greater of Chicot avg hourly wage OR $12.50/hr**. The 125% bonus trigger is different from the entry threshold.
- **R&D credits cover wages + fringe ONLY** — not supplies, equipment, or cloud compute. Earlier framing was too broad.
- **Targeted Business designation is not automatic** — requires $250K equity + 150% wage threshold + sector fit. LLRC at $500 capital starts with **Strategic Value R&D (33%, non-transferable)** until equity bridges the gap.
- **Arkansas Rural Community Grant is NOT applicable to LLCs** — it funds municipal infrastructure. Strike from strategy.
- **InvestArk and ArkPlus are low-leverage** for a services R&D LLC; skip unless capital equipment spend exceeds ~$500K.
- **LA IRG cap appears lower than earlier stated** — 25% of federal award up to $50K, not flat $100K. Verify with LED before relying on higher figure.
