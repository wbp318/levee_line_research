## Arkansas Highlights (Leading Path B)

Full detail in [Arkansas Expansion](arkansas/11_ARKANSAS_EXPANSION.md). This is the at-a-glance reference.

### Why Arkansas

1. **Research infrastructure is on the AR side.** Rohwer Research Station (Desha County, ~30 mi north of Lake Village) — 634 ac, 175 field trials/year, 18,000 plots/year — UADA's Delta row-crop hub. UAM's new $16.8M **Arkansas Forest Health Research Center** opens Summer 2026. UA Division of Agriculture operates 11 research stations statewide. Nothing comparable south of the river.
2. **1890 land-grant pipeline via UAPB.** Only AR institution eligible for Evans-Allen capacity formula, **1890 Capacity Building Grants ($100K–$750K awards, $28M annual pool)**, 1890 Centers of Excellence, 1890 Scholarships. LA has no 1890 equivalent.
3. **NSF EPSCoR + DART thematic fit.** Arkansas's active $20M NSF EPSCoR Track-1 is **DART — Data Analytics that are Robust and Trusted** — the exact trust/interpretability layer applied AI-ag needs. Both AR and LA are EPSCoR; AR's current flagship project matches our domain.
4. **Chicot County is Tier 4 distressed.** Max AEDC incentive tier: 4% Advantage Arkansas payroll credit ($50K min payroll threshold), Create Rebate, ArkPlus, Targeted Business R&D.
5. **Targeted sector state for NAICS 541715.** Unlocks **33% Targeted Business R&D tax credit** ($50K/yr × 5 yrs; transferable once) + **33⅓% Equity Investment Tax Credit** (transferable once).
6. **Lower tax surface.** Flat $150/yr franchise; top individual rate 3.9% (vs LA 4.25%); PTET election available (4.9% ordinary / 2.45% cap gains — bypasses federal $10K SALT cap).

### ⚠️ Advantage Arkansas — Caveat for Owner-Operators

Two walls block self-hire from qualifying:
1. **Partnership tax (Rev. Rul. 69-184)** — partners can't be W-2 employees of their own partnership; "wages" get recharacterized as guaranteed payments on the K-1. Workaround: S-corp election (Form 2553), but that has its own costs.
2. **AR taxpayer requirement** — AEDC requires employees on the payroll count to be **AR taxpayers**. Until AR residency is established, a LA-resident Managing Member's wages don't count.

**Budget Advantage AR around year-2 AR-resident FTE hires (data scientist, field tech, agronomist), NOT around self-payroll.**
