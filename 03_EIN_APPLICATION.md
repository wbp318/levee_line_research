# EIN Application Guide — Levee Line Research LLC

Apply online at the IRS — it's free and you get your EIN immediately.

**URL:** https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online
**Hours:** Monday-Friday, 7am-10pm ET
**Cost:** Free
**Time:** ~15 minutes, EIN issued instantly

---

## Before You Apply

You need your Articles of Organization to be filed and approved first. Have the following ready:

- Louisiana Secretary of State file number (from Articles approval)
- Your SSN (as the responsible party — **do NOT commit to git**)
- Business formation date (from Articles)

## Tax Classification — Important

### Default: Partnership
A multi-member LLC is automatically classified as a **partnership** for federal tax purposes.
- The Company files **Form 1065** (U.S. Return of Partnership Income)
- Each Member receives a **Schedule K-1** showing their share of income, deductions, and credits
- Members report their K-1 amounts on their personal tax returns (Form 1040)

### Alternative: S-Corp Election
You can elect S-Corp taxation by filing **Form 2553** with the IRS.
- Must file within 75 days of formation, or by March 15 for the following tax year
- Potentially saves self-employment tax if the Company generates significant income
- Requires paying yourself a "reasonable salary" (with payroll taxes)
- **Recommendation:** Stay as partnership for now. Revisit S-Corp election once revenue exceeds ~$50,000/year

### Grant Implications
- Partnership taxation is standard and acceptable for all federal grant programs
- SBIR/STTR programs do not restrict based on tax classification
- Keep clean books — grant auditors will examine how funds flow to members

## After You Receive Your EIN

1. **Print/save the EIN confirmation letter (CP 575)** — you'll need this for:
   - Bank account opening
   - SAM.gov registration
   - Grant applications
   - State tax registration
2. **Store securely** — treat the EIN like sensitive business information
3. **Next steps:**
   - Open a business bank account (see [05_BANK_ACCOUNT_SETUP.md](05_BANK_ACCOUNT_SETUP.md))
   - Register on SAM.gov (see [06_FEDERAL_REGISTRATION.md](06_FEDERAL_REGISTRATION.md))
   - Register with Louisiana Department of Revenue
