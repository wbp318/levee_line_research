## Phase 1 — Form the LLC

- [ ] **Search and verify business name availability**
  - Search: https://www.sos.la.gov/BusinessServices/SearchForBusinessFilings/Pages/default.aspx
  - Confirm "Levee Line Research LLC" is available

- [ ] **File Articles of Organization with Louisiana Secretary of State**
  - File online: https://www.sos.la.gov/BusinessServices/FileBusinessDocuments/Pages/default.aspx
  - Filing fee: $100 (online) or $75 (mail)
  - Initial report fee: $25 (filed simultaneously)
  - See [01_ARTICLES_OF_ORGANIZATION.md](01_ARTICLES_OF_ORGANIZATION.md) for all fields
  - Decision: file with just Managing Member now, amend to add members later (recommended)

- [ ] **Create Operating Agreement**
  - Louisiana does not require filing, but essential for multi-member LLC
  - See [02_OPERATING_AGREEMENT.md](02_OPERATING_AGREEMENT.md)
  - All members must sign
  - Defines manager powers, profit splits, voting, transfers

- [ ] **Get a certified copy of your Articles**
  - Request from Secretary of State after approval ($30)
  - Needed for: bank account, SAM.gov, grant applications

## Phase 2 — Federal Tax ID

- [ ] **Apply for EIN (Employer Identification Number)**
  - Free at https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online
  - Apply as "Limited Liability Company"
  - Multi-member LLC: taxed as **partnership** by default (Form 1065)
  - Print/save the EIN confirmation letter (CP 575)
  - See [03_EIN_APPLICATION.md](03_EIN_APPLICATION.md)

## Phase 3 — Federal Grant Registration (START IMMEDIATELY)

**This phase takes 2-4 weeks. Begin as soon as you have your EIN.**

- [ ] **Register on SAM.gov**
  - https://sam.gov/content/entity-registration
  - Create Login.gov account
  - Receive your UEI (Unique Entity Identifier)
  - Processing: 7-10 business days
  - See [06_FEDERAL_REGISTRATION.md](06_FEDERAL_REGISTRATION.md)

- [ ] **Register on Grants.gov**
  - https://www.grants.gov/register
  - Requires active SAM.gov registration
  - Processing: 1-3 business days after SAM.gov
  - Set up email alerts for USDA SBIR, NSF SBIR, DOE SBIR

- [ ] **Register on SBA / SBIR.gov**
  - https://www.sbir.gov/registration
  - Certify as small business

- [ ] **Create Research.gov account**
  - https://www.research.gov
  - Required for NSF SBIR/STTR Project Pitches

- [ ] **Contact Louisiana LTTO**
  - https://www.opportunitylouisiana.gov/ltto
  - Request $5,000 SBIR/STTR proposal preparation assistance
  - Do this NOW — help with writing your first proposal

## Phase 4 — Louisiana State Requirements

- [ ] **Register with Louisiana Department of Revenue**
  - https://latap.revenue.louisiana.gov/
  - Required for state income tax

- [ ] **Occupational License / Business Permit**
  - Contact East Carroll Parish Police Jury: (318) 559-2256
  - 400 First Street, Lake Providence, LA 71254
  - Ask about requirements for a research LLC

- [ ] **File Annual Report with Secretary of State**
  - Due every year on formation anniversary
  - $30 fee (online)
  - Missing this can dissolve your LLC
  - See [04_LOUISIANA_COMPLIANCE.md](04_LOUISIANA_COMPLIANCE.md)

## Phase 5 — Banking

- [ ] **Open a business bank account**
  - Bring: EIN letter, Articles of Organization, Operating Agreement, driver's license
  - Multi-member LLC: decide who has signatory authority (see [05_BANK_ACCOUNT_SETUP.md](05_BANK_ACCOUNT_SETUP.md))
  - Local options: Citizens Bank & Trust (Lake Providence), Tensas State Bank
  - Online options: Mercury (recommended), Relay, Bluevine
  - Get a business debit card

- [ ] **Set up bookkeeping**
  - QuickBooks, Wave (free), or spreadsheet
  - Track: grant funds, capital contributions, research expenses, equipment, travel

- [ ] **Set up EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer)**
  - Required for receiving federal grant payments
  - Configured during SAM.gov registration using your business bank info

## Phase 6 — Funding Applications

- [ ] **Apply for SBA Microloan** (up to $50K for bridge capital)
- [ ] **Submit NSF SBIR Project Pitch** (when portal reopens April-May 2026)
- [ ] **Watch for USDA SBIR solicitation** (expected May-June 2026)
- [ ] **Contact Louisiana Growth Fund partners** (equity investment $250K-$1M)
- [ ] **Explore Louisiana SSBCI programs** (loans, guarantees, seed capital)
- [ ] **Apply for LA R&D Tax Credit** (after July 1, 2026)
- [ ] **Apply for Innovation Retention Grant** (after receiving SBIR/STTR award)
- See [07_FUNDING_ROADMAP.md](07_FUNDING_ROADMAP.md) and [08_GRANT_CALENDAR.md](08_GRANT_CALENDAR.md) for full details

## Phase 7 — Ongoing Compliance

- [ ] **Louisiana Annual Report** — due every year on formation anniversary ($30)
- [ ] **Federal tax return** — multi-member LLC files Form 1065 (partnership return)
  - Each member gets a Schedule K-1
  - Members report their share on personal returns
- [ ] **Louisiana state income tax return** — file with LA Department of Revenue
- [ ] **Self-employment tax** — 15.3% on net earnings (Managing Member)
- [ ] **Estimated quarterly taxes** — due April 15, June 15, September 15, January 15
- [ ] **SAM.gov annual renewal** — must renew every year to stay grant-eligible
- [ ] **Keep records for 7 years** — especially grant-related expenses and reports
